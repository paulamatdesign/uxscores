## Items

7 points Likert scales. Items represents 2 dimensions.

### Pragmatic Quality

- Q1: obstructive - supportive
- Q2: complicated - easy
- Q3: inefficient - efficient
- Q4: confusing - clear

### Hedonic Quality

- Q5: boring - exciting
- Q6: not interesting - interesting
- Q7: conventional - inventive
- Q8: usual - leading edge

## Transform

- Rescales the data to the range -3 to 3.
- For each dimension, compute mean and other descriptives.
